Quick Tips

  • Click visible text in the preview to edit it directly.
  • Drag an image inside its frame to change the crop position.
  • Drop a new image or video file onto a media frame to replace it.
  • Use Export Site to download `index.html`, `site.css`, and `site-data.json`.
